# Operations

This section covers day-to-day usage of the AlphaOracle System — how to manage trading activity, respond to system events, and stay informed about what the system is doing.

## In This Section

- [Trade Approvals](trade-approvals.md) — Review and approve proposed orders in MANUAL_APPROVAL mode before they are submitted to your broker
- [Kill Switch](kill-switch.md) — Emergency stop that immediately halts all trading activity and cancels open orders
- [Monitoring & Alerts](monitoring-alerts.md) — Dashboard, Prometheus/Grafana metrics, and Slack/Telegram alert channels for tracking system health and risk events
